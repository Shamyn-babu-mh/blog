How i got my driving license in Kochi, Kerala, India

Published on: July 10, 2026



It all started with my brother and my mother pushing me into getting a driving license. I looked up for a driving school near me in google maps. Found one and joined.

I was told that first I would have to apply for a learner's license and only then I would be eligible to get a driving license.

My agent took care of my application process. During the application process, I gave him the below mentioned documents:
- Aadhar card.
- Birth Certificate.
I was also asked my blood group. I think during the online application process, I recieved an OTP in my contact number, which I think I passed on to my agent.

After this I got two documents. First document was called FORM 2 and the second document was called "New LL Acknowledgement". You can see these documents below.
Take a printout of just the 6th page of Form 2 and the New LL Acknowledgement and go to an Optholmologist for your test certificate. This eye test certificate is neccessary to move forward. As per the instruction in the last page of "Form 2 document", the eye test certificate is to be issued by an Optholmologist with a registered post graduate certificate in Optholmology. Also, before going to the optholmologist, stick a passport size photo of yours in the "Form 6" printout that you have. I have shown below the certificate that I recieved from my Optholmologist. After this, I sent this eye certificate to my agent. He then, applied for my learner's license online. I was given a pdf that had 500 questions and the answers to them. I was informed to learn these. So, during the learner's license test, they ask us 30 questions out of these 500 questions. Out of these 30 questions, one would have to answer 18 questions correctly to pass the test. During my test, I realised that learnign questions regarding "road signs" we extremely important. I think i was asked around 22 questions in my test and out of that, around half of the questions were regarding road signs (they showed a road sign and asked what it meant). By the way, for my learner's driver's test, I went to the Regional Transport Office in Kakkanad (Address: 2nd Floor, Civil Station, Kakkanad, Thrikakkara PO, 682030). The time and date of the exam was informed to me by the agent (I think he booked the time slot for my LL exam, online).
